# Explore the brand palette

A dedicated **Lucid Labs** view container in the activity bar gives you a live colour palette browser.

## What's in it

- Every semantic role from the active variant (e.g. `accent`, `keyword`, `string`, `terminal.cyan`)
- Each row shows the role name, the hex value, and a swatch
- **Click any row to copy the hex to your clipboard**

## Theme Reference webview

The button in the description opens a full-page **Theme Reference** — a side-by-side preview of the brand palette in context. Useful for:

- Brand reviews
- Customising your own derivative themes
- Picking a hex for a Figma file, dashboard, or document

The reference automatically renders in the active variant — toggle dark/light and reopen to see the alternate.
